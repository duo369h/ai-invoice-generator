## Dashboard Principle

Dashboard language should describe workflow state, document state, and client communication state.

Avoid:

```text
Paid
Revenue
Payment link delivered
Transfer recorded
Workspace balance updated
Get paid
Never miss a payment
```

Use:

```text
Approved
Completed
Delivered
Client Updated
Workflow Updated
Timeline Updated
```
