## What Corvioz Is

Corvioz is a freelancer workflow and client delivery workspace that helps independent professionals organize quotes, proposals, client documents, client records, and delivery history in one focused system. It gives freelancers and small studios a structured way to manage client-facing work, prepare professional documents, and keep project context clear from first request to final delivery. Paid plan checkout is handled separately by Paddle when a user chooses to upgrade.
