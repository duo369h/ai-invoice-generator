## Approved Product Description (Compliance-Safe)

Corvioz is a Freelancer Workflow System and client delivery workspace for independent professionals, consultants, designers, developers, photographers, and small studios. It helps users organize quotes, proposals, invoices, client documents, public profiles, client records, review status, document exports, and client delivery workflows in one focused workspace. Corvioz is not a payment processor, billing platform, accounting system, tax product, or money movement service. Paid plan checkout is handled by Paddle as the secure checkout provider.
